Digital Millennium Copyright Act Repeat Infringer Termination Policy

9p does not have subscribers, patrons, nor account holders; 9p has no means to identify and cannot identify the IP addresses of individuals who send communications over our network; nonetheless in accordance with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA), it is our policy to terminate the use of our services by repeat infringers in appropriate circumstances.


Digital Millennium Copyright Act Safe Harbor

The Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) offers four "safe harbors" to protect ISPs from copyright liability for the acts of their users, provided that certain requirements are met (17 U.S.C. § 512). 9p is fully compliant viä section 512(a) and is a section 512(a) "conduit provider".


Requests Of Subpoena To Identify Infringer

9p keeps no logs of any kind; 9p has no means to and cannot identify the IP addresses of individuals who send communications over our network; nonetheless, 9p will fully comply and capitulate with the legal requests of any law enforcement agency.
